About μPACS

μPACS is a lightweight Picture Archiving and Communication System designed for storing, retrieving, and managing medical imaging data in DICOM format.

The system follows the standard DICOM hierarchy: Patient → Study → Series → Instance

  • Patient: Individual person who is the subject of medical imaging
  • Study: A single imaging examination (e.g., CT scan, MRI)
  • Series: A collection of images from a specific acquisition
  • Instance: Individual DICOM image file
